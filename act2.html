Chicken curry recipe

ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken(cut into pieces,bone-in for flavor)
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 onion(chopped)
  • 3 garlic cloves(minced)
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger(grated)
  • 2 tbsp curry powder(adjust for spiciness
  • 1 can(400ml)coconut milk
  • 1 cup water or chicken broth
  • 2 potatoes(peeled,cubed)
  • 1 carrot(peeled,sliced)
  • 1 bell pepper(sliced,any color)
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce(patis)
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 chili pepper(sliced,for heat)

Instructions:

    heat oil in a pot over medium heat.fry onion until soft(2 mins),then add garlic and ginger.cook until fragrant(1 min) add chicken pieces and brown on all sides(5-7 mins) stir in curry powder and coat chicken evenly.cook for 1 min to release its flavor pour in coconut milk and water/broth.Bring to a boil.then reduce heat to low,Cover and simmer for 10 mins Toss in potatoes,carrots,and optional chili.Cover and cook for 15-20 mins until chicken is tender and veggies are soft Stir in fish sauce,bell pepper,salt,and pepper.Simmer for 2 more mins Pair with steamed rice.Enjoy!
